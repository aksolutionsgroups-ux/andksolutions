Refund Policy

Last updated: October 16, 2025

Overview

We aim for your complete satisfaction. This policy outlines when refunds may be available and how we handle cancellations.

Eligibility

  • Advance payments are typically non-refundable once work begins.
  • If deliverables do not match agreed scope, we will work to rectify or offer a partial refund where appropriate.
  • Custom software work is generally non-refundable due to time and materials spent.

Cancellations

  • Client-initiated cancellations are handled case-by-case depending on completed milestones.
  • If we must cancel due to unforeseen circumstances, we will refund amounts for undelivered services.

How to Request a Refund

Contact us at aksolutionsgroups@gmail.com with your project details. We will review and respond within 3 business days.
